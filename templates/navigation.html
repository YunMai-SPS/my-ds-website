ABOUT

Hi, my name is Yun and I work as a data scientist. I hold a master's degree in data science (City University of New York) and a Ph.D. in biochemistry and molecular biology (Chinese Academy of Science).

I have enjoyed solving problems by collecting and analyzing data since my after-graduate program, much like a skilled origamist, crafting intricate patterns from data with precision and logic, and folding complexity into elegant solutions.

With a mixed background of more than 10 years of experience in molecular biology and biochemistry research and more than 3 years of data analytics, natural language processing, and computational biology in precision medicine on cancer and Mendelian disease, I have collaborated closely with pharmaceutical clients which gave me the ability to translate their business objectives into specific and measurable project goals.
